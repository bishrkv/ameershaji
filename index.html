My Story

From a Learning to
Leading

Ameer Shaji K is a Social Entrepreneur, HR Professional, Trainer, and Career Counselor dedicated to empowering people through education, leadership, and personal growth. His journey spans administration, human resources, and entrepreneurship, where he has consistently focused on developing talent and creating meaningful opportunities.

After building extensive experience in management and HR leadership, Ameer founded Skoolguru, an innovative e-learning platform aimed at transforming education through technology and purpose-driven learning. He later expanded his vision through Skool Box, further contributing to the educational ecosystem.

Today, Ameer continues to inspire students, professionals, and aspiring leaders through mentoring, training, and entrepreneurship, driven by a simple mission:
